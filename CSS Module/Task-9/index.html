Community Bulletin

The annual summer fair is coming up this July, featuring live music, food vendors, and local artisans. Attendees can enjoy family-friendly activities, participate in contests, and explore exhibitions from local talent.

In other news, the City Council approved funding for new park renovations across the district. Renovation work will begin next month, starting with Central Green Park, which will receive new playground equipment and improved walking trails.

Volunteers are also encouraged to register for this year's "Clean-Up Day", which helps maintain public spaces and foster civic pride. For more information, visit the council's website or contact your local community liaison.
